The Ecosystem Metrics page provides comprehensive insights into the performance and growth of the XRAY/Network ecosystem. Here, users can explore various statistics and data visualizations that highlight key metrics such as user adoption, transaction volumes, liquidity pool statistics, staking activities, and more. This information is crucial for understanding the health and trajectory of the XRAY ecosystem, enabling users and developers to make informed decisions based on real-time data.
