# Privacy Policy

XRAY/Network ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services, including our Wallet, Staking platform, Mini Apps, and Extensions (collectively, the "Services"). Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

- <strong>Blockchain Data</strong>: As our Services interact with public blockchain networks, your wallet address,
  transactions, and balances may be collected and used as part of providing the Services. This information is public by
  design and beyond our control.
- <strong>Staking Information</strong>: If you participate in staking, we may collect staking-related data, such as
  wallet addresses and transaction details.
- <strong>Support Requests</strong>: When you contact support, we may collect the information you provide, including
  your contact details and the nature of your inquiry.
- <strong>Device Information</strong>: We collect data about the device and software you use to access our Services,
  including IP address, operating system, browser type, and app version.
- <strong>Usage Data</strong>: Information about how you interact with our Services, including features used, pages
  visited, and actions taken.
- <strong>Third-Party Integrations</strong>: If you connect third-party accounts or services (e.g., DApps or
  extensions), we may receive certain information as permitted by those services.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our Services.
- Facilitate wallet transactions and staking activities.
- Enhance security and prevent fraud.
- Respond to your inquiries and provide support.
- Conduct analytics to better understand our users and improve the user experience.
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

- With Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our Services, such as hosting, analytics, and customer support.
- With Third-Party Apps and Integrations: If you choose to interact with third-party mini-apps or extensions through our platform, your information may be shared with those third parties as needed.
- For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or government requests.
- As Required by Law: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests by public authorities.
- For Security and Fraud Prevention: To protect our rights, users, and Services, we may share information to prevent or investigate security breaches or fraudulent activities.

## 4. Your Rights and Choices

- You can manage privacy preferences within the settings of our Wallet, Staking platform, Mini Apps, or Extensions.
- To stop data collection, uninstall our apps or disconnect third-party integrations.

## 5. Data Retention

- We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

## 6. Third-Party Links and Services

- Our Services may include links to third-party websites, apps, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through our Services or by other means.
