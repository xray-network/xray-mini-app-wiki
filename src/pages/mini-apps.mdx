# Mini Apps

Mini Apps are lightweight, modular applications seamlessly integrated into the XRAY ecosystem, enabling users and developers to extend the platform's functionality effortlessly. Designed with versatility and user experience in mind, Mini Apps offer tailored solutions for a wide range of needs, from custom DeFi tools to niche utilities that enhance the core features of XRAY/App.

By leveraging our open-source Mini Apps technology, developers can create and deploy their own applications directly within XRAY/App. These apps integrate smoothly, allowing users to access innovative tools and services without leaving the ecosystem. Whether it's specialized financial calculators, interactive utilities, or unique staking strategies, Mini Apps empower users to unlock new opportunities within the decentralized finance space.

For users, Mini Apps mean customization and convenience. Access specialized tools and features designed to enhance your experience, all within the secure and seamless XRAY environment. Developers benefit from an intuitive framework that simplifies app creation, ensuring they can focus on delivering value.

With Mini Apps, XRAY/App transforms into a dynamic and expandable platform, fostering a collaborative ecosystem where innovation thrives. This approach ensures XRAY remains adaptable, future-proof, and ready to meet the evolving needs of its growing community.
